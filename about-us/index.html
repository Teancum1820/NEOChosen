About the Kirtland Heritage Group

The Kirtland Heritage Group is a Northeast Ohio-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing people together through faith, community, service, and uplifting events. While our roots are in Kirtland, Ohio, our mission reaches far beyond one city. We strive to strengthen relationships across Northern Ohio by creating experiences that unite people of all backgrounds through positive, family-centered gatherings and community outreach.

Our organization believes that faith and community work best when they work together. Through concerts, devotionals, service projects, educational programs, civic events, and interfaith partnerships, we seek to build bridges between congregations, organizations, schools, and communities throughout the region.

Over the past several years, the Kirtland Heritage Group has successfully hosted large-scale interfaith and community events that have brought together tens of thousands of individuals from a wide variety of faith traditions and backgrounds. One of our recent events featuring cast members from The Chosen united representatives from 158 different congregations across Northern Ohio and created relationships that continue today.

We have been honored to host and partner with nationally recognized performers, speakers, and guests including:

  • The Piano Guys
  • Rudy Ruettiger
  • Kirby Heyborne
  • Nashville Tribute Band
  • The Bonner Family
  • Cast members from The Chosen

These events are designed not only to entertain, but to inspire faith, strengthen families, encourage service, and create lasting community connections.

In addition to major events, the Kirtland Heritage Group dedicates thousands of volunteer hours each year to civic and community projects throughout the area. Our volunteers support local schools, community beautification efforts, service initiatives, holiday activities, and public events that help strengthen Northern Ohio communities.

Each year, we organize community traditions including our annual 5K and Fun Walk, family events, concerts, and seasonal celebrations. During the Christmas season, volunteers from our organization help decorate portions of Kirtland with Christmas lights and displays to help create a welcoming and uplifting environment for residents and visitors alike.

Everything we do is centered around one goal: bringing people together in meaningful ways that uplift communities, strengthen relationships, and inspire hope.

We are grateful for the many churches, businesses, volunteers, and community members who continue to support our mission and partner with us in making a positive impact throughout Northern Ohio.
